Thomas Grogan

Performing Artist

Bio

Thomas is a trained singer from Cashel, Co. Tipperary. He performs music from a range of genres. His specialities are musical theatre and classical music. He has played leading roles in many musical productions. These include 'Jesus' in Jesus Christ Superstar, 'Jean Valjean' in Les Miserables and 'The Phantom' in Phantom of the Opera. Thomas performs regularly in concert settings and is available to sing for weddings, funerals, and other occassions. He holds a first class honours Arts degree from University College Cork.
